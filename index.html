This page does HTTP/HTTPS reachability checks, not true ICMP ping. Browser-only monitoring is limited by cross-origin rules, so this works best with URLs that serve a normal public page or favicon. For internal apps or deeper health checks, swap the target URL to your own lightweight health endpoint such as /health or /status.
